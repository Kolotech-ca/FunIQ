Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at FunIQ.ca

Our Commitment

At funIQ.ca, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our students, parents, and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you sign up or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, and other information you voluntarily provide.

Usage Information

We may collect information about your usage of the site, such as pages visited, activities completed, and worksheets accessed, to improve your learning experience.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our educational services
  • Send updates, newsletters, or responses to inquiries
  • Personalize the learning experience for students
  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of our website

Sharing Your Information

funIQ.ca does not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations. We may share anonymized data for analytics or educational research purposes.

Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. While we strive to keep your data safe, no method of transmission over the Internet can guarantee 100% security.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. For inquiries or requests regarding your data, please contact us via email.

Children’s Privacy

funIQ.ca is designed for students in grades 1–6. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the latest update will be reflected here.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@funIQ.ca.
