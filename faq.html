Frequently Asked Questions

What grades does funIQ.ca cover? ▼
funIQ.ca is designed for students in grades 1 through 6, covering Math, Science, English, and Social Studies.
How many activities and worksheets are available? ▼
We offer hundreds of pages and thousands of interactive activities, plus affordable worksheets for each subject.
Can I purchase worksheets individually? ▼
Yes! Worksheets can be purchased individually at low rates, making it easy for parents or teachers to use them at home or in the classroom.
Is funIQ.ca suitable for self-learning? ▼
Absolutely! funIQ.ca is designed for self-paced learning, allowing students to explore and practice independently.
How can I contact funIQ for support? ▼
You can contact us via email at info@funIQ.ca or connect with us on social media (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram).
